About Me

I am an empirical researcher working at the intersection of labor economics, the economics of crime and corrections, the economics of education, and racial/socioeconomic inequality.

Currently, I serve as a Research Associate at the Institute for Research on Labor and Employment (IRLE) under the mentorship of Professor Steven Raphael, and as a Pre-doctoral Research Fellow at the Democracy Policy Lab (DPL) working alongside Professor Jacob Grumbach at UC Berkeley. I hold a B.A. in Economics from UC Berkeley (2024), where I was a Latinx Research Center Fellow, and am an alumnus of the AEA Summer Training Program Advanced Track at Howard University.
